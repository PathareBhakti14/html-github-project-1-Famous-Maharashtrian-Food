Taste of Maharashtra

The Taste of Maharashtra is a rich and diverse cuisine characterized by a balance of spicy, sweet, and tangy flavors, heavily influenced by its geography and regional cultures. Popular dishes range from street food staples like Vada Pav and Misal Pav to coastal seafood curries, inland vegetarian lentil preparations, and celebratory sweets like Modak and Puran Poli.
