Purnpoli

Puran Poli is a traditional Indian sweet flatbread, popular in Maharashtra and South India, where it is known by various names such as Holige or Obbattu. It consists of a soft flatbread (poli) stuffed with a sweet filling (puran)typically made from chana dal (split chickpeas) & jaggery, flavored with aromatic spices.

Misal pav

Misal pav is a dish from the Indian state of Maharashtra. It consists of a vegetable curry, mostly made from, moth beans locally known as misal & pav, which is a type of Indian bread roll. The final dish is topped with farsan or sev, onions,lemon and coriander . It is usually served hot with bread or rolls toasted with butter and buttermilk or dahi and papad. Misal pav is served as a breakfast dish, as a snack and also as a full meal.

Vada pav

Vada pav is a popular vegetarian fast food from Maharashtra,India consisting of a deep-fried spicy potato fritter(Vada) served in a soft bread bun (Pav)with variousChutneys. It is often called an"Indian burger" and is a staple of Mumbai street food, known for it spicy and savory flavor
